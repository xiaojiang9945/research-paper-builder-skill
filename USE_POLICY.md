# Use Policy

This project may be used only for lawful, non-commercial, research-supportive, educational, or personal purposes.

## Allowed Uses

- Planning, drafting, revising, or checking legitimate research manuscripts.
- Improving clarity, structure, figure logic, terminology consistency, and submission readiness.
- Creating teaching examples, synthetic demos, and internal non-commercial research workflows.
- Auditing claims against user-provided source data and references.

## Prohibited Uses

Do not use this project to:

- fabricate data, references, author contributions, ethics approvals, peer reviews, or experimental results;
- disguise ghostwritten, purchased, or paper-mill manuscripts as legitimate author work;
- submit research text without author review and accountability;
- violate journal policies, institutional rules, research ethics, privacy law, copyright, or data-use agreements;
- process confidential, personal, patient, participant, reviewer, or collaborator data without permission and safeguards;
- generate misleading claims, unsupported medical/scientific conclusions, or deceptive submission materials;
- use the project commercially without separate written permission.

## User Responsibilities

Users must verify:

- all numerical claims against source data;
- all citations and bibliographic metadata;
- all figures and source-data mappings;
- all authorship, funding, ethics, data availability, and competing-interest statements;
- all journal-specific requirements before submission.

Generated text is drafting assistance, not a substitute for scientific judgment, author responsibility, legal review, or institutional approval.
