## Caption Draft

**Figure 1. Synthetic salt treatment reduces seed germination with genotype-dependent retention.**
(a) Day 7 germination percentage for three mock genotypes under water and salt treatment. Bars show mean +/- SD from three synthetic biological replicates, with 25 seeds per replicate.
(b) Day 1-7 germination trajectory under salt treatment.
(c) Day 7 salt-retention percentage, calculated as salt-treated Day 7 mean divided by water-treated Day 7 mean. All data are synthetic and are provided only as a demonstration of manuscript-package structure.
