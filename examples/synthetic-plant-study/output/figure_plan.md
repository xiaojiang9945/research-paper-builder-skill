## Caption Draft

**Figure 1. Synthetic seed germination package: design, endpoints, trajectory and claim boundary.**
(a) Schematic of the synthetic germination assay with three mock genotypes, two treatments, three biological replicates per genotype-treatment combination and 25 seeds per replicate.
(b) Day 7 germination percentage under water and salt treatment. Bars show mean +/- SD from three synthetic biological replicates.
(c) Day 1-7 germination trajectory under salt treatment.
(d) Day 7 salt-retention percentage, calculated as salt-treated Day 7 mean divided by water-treated Day 7 mean for each mock genotype.
(e) Evidence classes and claim boundary for the demo dataset.
(f) Manuscript-package outputs generated by the workflow. All values and references are synthetic and are provided only as a demonstration of manuscript-package structure.

## Visual QA Checklist

- One panel letter per panel.
- No duplicate panel letters.
- Axis labels include units.
- Legend terms match the terminology ledger.
- Source data are available for each plotted value.
- Schematic panels are clearly non-data panels.
- Captions define sample size, statistic and abbreviation.
