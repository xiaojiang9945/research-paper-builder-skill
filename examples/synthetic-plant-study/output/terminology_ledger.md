# Terminology Ledger

| concept | canonical_form | allowed_short_form | do_not_use | where_defined | notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| synthetic wild type | Mock-WT | WT only in figures after definition | real WT, Col-0 | Methods | fictional genotype |
| synthetic candidate line A | Mock-Line-A | Line A | tolerant line | Methods | avoid implying real tolerance |
| synthetic candidate line B | Mock-Line-B | Line B | resistant line | Methods | descriptive only |
| water control | water treatment | Water | H2O control | Methods | use Water in figures |
| salt treatment | salt treatment | Salt | NaCl stress if concentration is not specified | Methods | concentration is intentionally omitted in demo |
| endpoint | Day 7 germination percentage | Day 7 germination | final germination | Results | percentage = count / 25 seeds |
| retention metric | salt-retention percentage | retention | tolerance index | Results | Salt Day 7 mean / Water Day 7 mean |

## Forbidden Variants

- real accession names;
- real lab names;
- private project names;
- local folder names;
- mechanism labels such as "salt-resistance gene" or "validated pathway".
